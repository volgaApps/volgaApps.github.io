Picture Rebuild Text

Picture Rebuild Text is a puzzle game where the full image is restored step by step. Complete mini-puzzles, reveal new fragments, collect points, and rebuild the whole picture piece by piece. The game may use Google Play Games Services for sign-in, cloud save, and leaderboards. It may also display ads and collect anonymous analytics to improve gameplay stability and quality.
